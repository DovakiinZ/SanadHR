
# Saudi HR Cloud System — Product & Technical Structure

## Product Vision
Build a modern cloud-based HR SaaS platform for the Saudi market that gathers all major HR modules in one simple, fast, scalable, and Arabic-first system.

## Core Modules
- Core HR
- Employee Self-Service (ESS)
- Custom Requests & Workflow Builder
- Attendance Management
- Payroll
- Expenses
- Loans
- ATS & Recruitment
- Reports
- Documents & Forms
- Notifications
- SaaS Admin Management
